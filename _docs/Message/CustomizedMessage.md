与好友分享图片！发送计算机、手机或相机中的照片和图片。

> Images are stored until you choose to remove them.

To share an image:

1. Open a message with someone
2. Select the **Send Media** button
3. Pick an image

![](//placehold.it/800x600)

Supported types:

* JPEG
* PNG
* BMP
* GIF

Unsupported types:

* SVG
* WEBP
